**Cauchy-Riemann**   柯西-黎曼方程
$f(z)=u(x,y)+iv(x,y)$ 
$$\begin{align}
\dfrac{\partial u}{\partial x}&=\;\;\dfrac{\partial v}{\partial y} \\
\dfrac{\partial u}{\partial y}&=-\dfrac{\partial v}{\partial x}    
\end{align}$$
